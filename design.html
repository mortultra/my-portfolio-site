Instdio Communications

An ongoing project that began with creating the logo and brand guidelines for the Toronto-based communications consultancy. The project includes a style guide, which is currently being applied to an array of business and brand applications in development. Designed in collaboration with Stephanie Penev.

Explor VR

Explor VR is Travelweek’s virtual reality travel platform for travel companies and tourism boards to preview their packages and destinations. Explor VR’s collateral required a brand mark and animated lock-up, as well as print and digital ads created with partner organizations.

Dinowash

Comprehensive, ground-up branding for a car detailing service, prior to its launch on the Apple and Google Play stores. Across a 5-week timeline I created a primary logo and wordmark, application assets, print and digital advertising, uniforms, office materials and a WordPress website.

Travelweek

Produced a 24-40 page issue of the magazine on a weekly basis, encompassing page count to layout, image selection, pre-flight and production management. Completed a comprehensive redesign of the magazine in 2019. Along with the redesign, 17 new custom layouts were created for advertiser-sponsored content. Maintained company media kits and created digital editions for each issue of the magazine and partner publications, as well as e-blasts, digital and print advertisements and other digital assets.

Japan/Tokyo 2018

Created in partnership with GoTokyo and TokyoTokyo (the official Tokyo tourism board), I led the design and production of the Tokyo/Japan 2018 City Guide. The 16-page B2B publication highlighting the city’s features and visitor incentives was geared towards the travel industry, and resulted in a statistically-significant increase in bookings among surveyed partner agencies.
