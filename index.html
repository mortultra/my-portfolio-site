Travelweek

Travelweek is a Canadian travel trade news organization.

Deliverables: weekly 24-48 page print magazine, digital edition, marketing materials, and advertising for clients. 2019 saw the magazine refreshed with new layouts, design elements and typography choices.

The Japan/Tokyo 2018 is an information guide for travel agents to familiarize themselves with Japan and Tokyo. Project clients were Go Tokyo the Japan Tourism Agency.
